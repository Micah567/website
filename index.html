Checkers

Place your checkers on the the first 3 rows of black squares on a chess board.

Checkers can only move diagonally forward. Not straight forward, backwards, sideways or diagonally backwards.

to take checkers you have to be in a position where your checker is in front of your opponents checker and there is a empty square behind it you go to the empty space and take the opponents checker
